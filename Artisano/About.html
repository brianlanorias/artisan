About Us

Just a month ago we looked for artisans to make our school uniforms, but
as we were doing so, we observed a significant shift in the crafts industry.
We have noticed that craftsmen are becoming increasingly hard to find,
and their trades are progressively fading. We realized that both the artisans
and the customers are having difficulty selling their goods and finding them
for their services. And so, the idea of coming up with a solution to this issue
captured our attention. From this idea, the website known as "Artisano" was
born, with the goal of bridging the gap between customers
and artisans and helping them to grow their businesses.
