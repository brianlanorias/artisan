Damaso Cuello

When I was a teenager, my father frequently took me to visit one of his friends who worked as a blacksmith. I was always curious about how they did it, so I watched and tried to pick it up. Later, when I was old enough to work on my own, my father's friend showed me how to do it. And ever since I began shaping steel and iron, it has both served as a hobby and a means of support. You can get in touch with me if you need backsmith, and I'll make sure you love it as much as how I love doing it.
